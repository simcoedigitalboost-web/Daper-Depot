Our Story

Decades of dressing Orillia's gentlemen with quality, service, and style.

The Dapper in Dapper Depot

Steve Orr isn't just the owner and proprietor of Dapper Depot Menswear. He is the dapper. Once you meet Steve, you'll understand what real service looks like.

Personable, knowledgeable, and genuinely passionate about helping men look their best, Steve brings decades of experience in men's fashion to every customer interaction. From the fabric to the fit, he guarantees satisfaction.

Come in, say hi. Swap stories about the old days. Then get dressed for a new day.

A Legacy of Quality

The heritage of Orr's Dapper Depot stretches back decades in Downtown Orillia. That commitment to quality menswear and personal attention continues today at 39 Mississaga Street East.

We carry Canadian-made labels that stand proudly shoulder to shoulder with their European and American counterparts. Brands like Paul Betenly, BLU by Polifroni, Marco Canadian Company, and Viyella represent the kind of craftsmanship that lasts.

In a world of fast fashion and disposable clothing, we believe in pieces that fit properly, wear beautifully, and become part of your story.

Our Promise

Personal Service, Every Time

No commission-hungry salespeople. No pressure tactics. Just honest advice from people who genuinely want you to look and feel your best. Whether you're shopping for a wedding, a job interview, or just refreshing your wardrobe, we're here to help.
